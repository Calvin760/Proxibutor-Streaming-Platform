About Us

Welcome to Proxibutor

At Proxibutor, we are proud to be the distributor for a vibrant and diverse roster of artists under our small record label. Our mission is to empower musicians by providing them with a platform to share their music, connect with fans, and collaborate with each other.

We believe in the power of community in the music industry. Our artists can easily stream each other’s music, fostering collaboration and inspiration. Whether you're an emerging artist or an established musician, our platform is designed to showcase your talent and help you reach a wider audience.

Our Artists

Each artist in our label brings their unique sound and perspective to the table. From soulful ballads to energetic beats, our collection spans a wide range of genres. We celebrate creativity and originality, and we are dedicated to supporting our artists in their musical journeys.

By streaming their music, you not only discover new sounds but also become a part of a community that values artistic expression and collaboration. Explore our collection, and find your new favorite artist today!

Join Us

Whether you are a music lover or an aspiring artist, we invite you to join us on this journey. Sign up to stay updated on new releases, exclusive content, and upcoming events. Together, let’s celebrate the art of music and support each other in our creative endeavors.

Thank you for being a part of Proxibutor! We can't wait for you to explore our music and discover the incredible talent within our label.
